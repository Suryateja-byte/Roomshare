## [2] (medium/logic-bug) noindex activeFilterCount diverges from chip count / countActiveFilters (the documented single source of truth)

This is the 5th ad-hoc counter the comment claimed to have eliminated.
- **repro**: Open /search?q=Austin&lat=30&lng=-97&minPrice=500&maxPrice=1500&amenities=Wifi. Inspect the rendered <meta name=robots> (index:false) — page is noindex with only 2 visible chips, while a page with 2 amenity chips + 1 room-type chip (3 real chips) and no bounds is treated identically. The two notions of 'highly filtered' do not agree.
